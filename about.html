Why we built this

At NVIDIA, Kevin watched teams spend $40K on user research for a developer tool — only to get a 40-page PDF that said "users found the onboarding complex." At Anthropic, Sarah realized that behavioral simulation at scale was technically possible, just nobody had applied it to product UX.

The insight: user testing isn't broken because it's hard to do — it's broken because the incentives are wrong. Recruiting real users takes weeks. You test when you have budget, not when you have questions. And the people you test are never the people who actually left.

Racoonn runs 5,000 AI personas — each built from real demographic and behavioral data — through your product. They arrive with different goals, different patience levels, different devices. They judge. They drop off. You get a report with ranked issues and specific fixes.

We use it on our own landing page every week. The version you're looking at right now has been tested by 5,000 AI users. (We found three things to fix.)
