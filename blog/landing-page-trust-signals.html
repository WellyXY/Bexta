14 Trust Signals That Boost Landing Page Conversions

Visitors don't buy from pages they don't trust. Trust signals are the elements that lower perceived risk and make your offer feel safe. Here are 14 that actually move conversion rates.

Social Proof Signals

Customer count ("10,000+ teams trust us"), named testimonials with photos, case study results ("Company X grew 3x"), review platform ratings (G2, Capterra), logos of recognizable customers. Each of these reduces the question: "Is this real?"

Risk Reduction Signals

Money-back guarantee, free trial (no credit card), transparent pricing, cancel-anytime policy, data security badges (SOC2, GDPR), refund rate disclosure. These answer: "What if I regret this?"

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a trust signal on a landing page?

Any element that reduces visitor skepticism — testimonials, review ratings, security badges, guarantees, customer logos, or usage statistics.

Do trust badges actually increase conversion?

Yes, when placed at the right friction point. Security badges near payment fields, testimonials near the CTA, and guarantee copy above the buy button are highest impact.

Can too many trust signals hurt conversion?

Yes. A page cluttered with badges and testimonials looks desperate. Pick your top 3-5 most relevant trust signals and display them cleanly.
