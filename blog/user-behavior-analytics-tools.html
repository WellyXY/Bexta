User Behavior Analytics Tools: Compared for 2026

Pageview analytics tells you how many people visited. Behavior analytics tells you what they did — and why they left. Here's how the top tools compare.

Session Recording Tools

Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity (free), FullStory, and LogRocket all record real user sessions. Clarity is unbeatable for budget teams. FullStory and LogRocket are better for debugging and error tracking. Hotjar sits in the middle with strong heatmap features alongside recordings.

Product Analytics Tools

Mixpanel, Amplitude, and Heap track user events throughout your product. These are better for understanding funnel drop-off in your app than on landing pages. They require more implementation effort but give deeper cohort analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is user behavior analytics?

User behavior analytics (UBA) tracks what users do on your site or app — where they click, how far they scroll, where they drop off. This goes beyond pageviews to understand the why behind your metrics.

Is Microsoft Clarity free?

Yes, Microsoft Clarity is completely free with no limits on sessions or users. It includes session recordings and heatmaps. The trade-off is less control over data privacy compared to paid alternatives.

What is the best user behavior analytics tool for small teams?

Microsoft Clarity for recordings and heatmaps (free). Google Analytics 4 for traffic data (free). A lightweight landing page testing tool for conversion insights. That combination covers 90% of what small teams need.
