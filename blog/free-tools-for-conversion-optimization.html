17 Free Conversion Optimization Tools for Landing Pages

You don't need a $1,000/month tool stack to optimize your landing page. Here are 17 free tools that cover every aspect of conversion optimization.

Free Analytics and Behavior Tools

Google Analytics 4 (free): traffic, sources, conversion events. Microsoft Clarity (free, unlimited): session recordings + heatmaps. Google Search Console (free): organic search performance. Hotjar free tier: 35 sessions/day, basic heatmaps. Facebook Pixel (free): conversion tracking for Facebook traffic.

Free Testing and Research Tools

Google Optimize (A/B testing, free). Lyssna free tier (5-second tests, first click tests). Maze free tier (user testing). UserTesting (limited free plan). Optimal Workshop Treejack (free tier for information architecture). Figma (free for individual designers — prototype testing).

Free Technical SEO and Speed Tools

Google PageSpeed Insights (Core Web Vitals + performance). GTmetrix free tier (waterfall load analysis). Google Lighthouse (Chrome DevTools — no signup needed). Screaming Frog free (crawl up to 500 URLs). Ahrefs Webmaster Tools (free backlink data for your own site).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free heatmap tool?

Microsoft Clarity is the best free heatmap tool — no session limits, no watermarks, and includes session recordings. It's genuinely enterprise-quality and completely free.

Is there a free A/B testing tool for landing pages?

Google Optimize was the standard, but it was discontinued in 2023. Current free options include Convert.com (free trial), VWO (limited free), or manual A/B tests using separate URLs and UTM parameters.

What are the most important free tools for a new landing page?

Priority order: Google Analytics 4 (traffic data), Microsoft Clarity (behavior data), Google Search Console (SEO performance), Google PageSpeed Insights (technical performance). These four give you a complete picture for free.
